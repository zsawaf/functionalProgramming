% Get all highways with cost less than 40. 
cheap(X,Y,Z) :- highway(X,Y,Z,Cost,_), Cost<40.

% Two different ways to get from toronto to timmins. 
highway(toronto,A,timmins,_,_), highway(toronto,B,timmins,_,_), A \= B.

% Note that this can also be defined using two different predicates, but I used
% an or to make it look shorter and prettier ;)
preferred(X,Y,Z):- cheap(X,Y,Z); highway(X,mountapu,Y,_,_).
